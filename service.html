Legal Help & Legal Aid

We provide Legal Help to those who qualify. To be enitled to Legal Help, you must either be on a passporting benefit or your income must be below the threshold. If you are employed we are able to assess your income to discover whether you do qualify. We provide Legal Aid to those who qualify for Legal Help. In order to obtain a Legal Aid certificate, the Legal Aid Agency must be satisfied with both the means and merits of every case provided to them.

Fixed Fee & Letter Packages

We offer Fixed Diagnostics which gives you an oppotunity to discuss your matter with us and in return we will provide you with one off advice. We also offer Fixed Fee Letter Packages which allows us to take on your matter administratively, by writing either 5 letters or an unlimited number of letters to the opponent
